about effervescent dream :

"At some point, we just got sick of everything...the abuse, the conflict, the fights-the sense of alienation and abandonment... We felt like our worlds were collapsing in around us and we were helpless, too helpless to stop any of it.

We wanted to share that with other kids who felt the same way.

We wanted to run away into the night.

We wanted to escape."

about the author :

lucifer (akumasaaan/luciferious), is a a webcomic creator, indie game dev, and freelancer based out of seattle, WA. ze has been passionate about the arts since ze was three years old. zir current on-going projects include the bloodscryer TTRPG, celestium life (life simulator game), and heart at war, a fantasy novel.

when lucifer is not working on zir projects, ze enjoys traveling, music, getting tattooed/pierced, reading, gaming, painting, making/modifying clothes, and learning languages.
